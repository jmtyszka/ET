Welcome to the home of ET

This Matlab package was originally developed to allow gaze estimation from videos of volunteers' eye movements recorded during functional MRI sessions at the Caltech Brain Imaging Center. The workflow has two phases: 1. Preparation for analysis. The video is edited for length, rotated if necessary, deinterlaced and MRI artifacts reduced, ready for actual pupilometry. 2. Video pupilometry and gaze estimation. A calibration and gaze tracking video are required for full gaze estimation.

There are currently two branches: a master branch designed for Windows 7 and a developmental branch for OS X Mavericks and Ubuntu.
